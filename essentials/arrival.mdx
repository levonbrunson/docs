# Arriving in Armenia

You've made it! Most arrivals are via **Zvartnots International Airport** (Yerevan) or **Gyumri Airport**. You may also arrive by land from Georgia or Iran.

## Zvartnots Airport to City Center
The airport is about 20 minutes from the city center.

### Transportation Options

| Option | Cost | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Taxi** | 2,000 - 6,000 AMD | Negotiate price if taking a personal taxi. Apps: GG, Yandex Taxi, Utaxi. |
| **Express Bus No. 201** | 300 AMD | Departs every 30 mins (07:00 - 22:00). Goes to Republic Square. Has WiFi. |
| **Public Bus No. 100** | Check current fares | Connects city center with airport. |

## Arriving by Land

### From Georgia
- **Route**: Via Bagratashen checkpoint.
- **Duration**: ~4 hours to Yerevan (221 km).
- **Train**: From Tbilisi to Sasuntsi Davit station (Yerevan). Takes ~10h but is comfortable.
- **Cost**: 7,000 - 25,000 AMD.

### From Iran
- **Duration**: ~7.5 hours from the border (380 km).
