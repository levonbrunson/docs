# Cost of Living in Armenia

Costs vary greatly based on lifestyle, but here are some estimates to help you budget.

## Monthly Budget Estimates (USD)

| Category | Single | Couple | Family |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rent** | $300 - 700 | $400 - 900 | $650 - 1,200 |
| **Utilities** | $30 - 70 | $50 - 100 | $80 - 200 |
| **Food** | $150 - 250 | $250 - 350 | $300 - 400 |
| **Transport** | $20 - 50 | $50 - 100 | $80 - 150 |
| **Healthcare** | $15 - 50 | $15 - 50 | $15 - 50 (per person) |
| **Lifestyle** | $100 - 300 | $200 - 500 | $300 - 600 |

## Expense Breakdown

### Food
*   **Groceries**: Affordable seasonal produce. Supermarkets range from budget to premium.
*   **Eating Out**: Business lunch ~$3-5. Dinner for two ~$20.

### Utilities
*   Includes electricity, gas, water, internet/TV/phone.
*   **Heating**: Decentralized (gas boilers) is common; costs vary by season and building insulation.
*   **Payment**: Easy via banking apps, terminals (Telcell, EasyPay), or post offices.

### Rent
*   Varies by location (center vs. outskirts) and building condition (new vs. old).
*   **Tip**: Consider sharing an apartment or living outside the center to save money.

### Transportation
*   **Public**: Buses, minibuses, Metro (Yerevan only). Affordable fares.
*   **Car**: Paid parking in zones A and B (15 mins free).
*   **Fuel**: Check [Numbeo](https://www.numbeo.com) for current prices.
