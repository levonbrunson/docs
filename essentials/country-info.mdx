# Country Information

Armenia, at 29,743 kmÂ², is situated on the border of Asia and Europe. It has two direct neighbors with open borders (Georgia and Iran) and two with closed borders.

## Key Facts

- **Capital**: Yerevan
- **Major Cities**: Gyumri, Vanadzor, Vagharshapat
- **Population**: ~3,067,400 (as of October 2024)
- **Yerevan Population**: ~1,098,900

## Demographics

The population is mostly homogeneous, with Armenians making up the absolute majority (98.1%). However, there are small minorities of:
- Russians
- Yezidis
- Kurds
- Assyrians
- Greeks
- Ukrainians
- Jews
- and others

## Trade Routes

Georgia and Iran serve as Armenia's main trade routes, with their borders open to import and export.
