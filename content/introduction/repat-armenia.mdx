# Your Repatriation Process with Us

At Repat Armenia, we define the repatriation as an individual's or family's decision made with accurate planning. We stand by you on a more intimate level, as you explore the possibilities, weigh the options, and embark on the journey to come, live and enjoy an engaging future in the homeland.

## About Us

Repat Armenia is a non-governmental, non-profit institution established in August 2012 with the aim of promoting active repatriation, informing, initiating and championing the return of high impact (professional, entrepreneurial) individuals and families. Full-time, dedicated staff based in our Yerevan office work, near Republic Square is ready to assist you in the following key areas:

- **Information support** (you will find a lot of practical information and tips about the process of moving, living and working in Armenia, personal experience and practical advice of repatriates and expats on our website and social networks)
- **Online and offline consultations** (orientation on integration, employment, doing business, networking, education, healthcare and legal aspects)
- **In-Depth Concierge Support Program**
- **Employment support** (profiles scanning, resume editing and job-matching activities)
- **Free Armenian language courses**
- **Medical insurance** (special package at a corporate price with 30% discount for 3 and more family members)
- **Networking & integration events** (informal meetings with fellow repatriates, acquaintance with interesting organizations and initiatives, workshops and training sessions, outgoing tours)

At Repat Armenia, we pay special attention to raising awareness and importance of repatriation through organizing forums in key diaspora communities, regular online talks and panel discussions on different aspects of repatriation and integration.

We also contribute to the development of a pro-repatriation environment and influencing government policies.

Once you become a part of our community, you can also be engaged in the process through sharing your personal and professional experience in Armenia.
