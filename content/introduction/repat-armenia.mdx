# Your Repatriation Process with Us

At Repat Armenia, we define repatriation as an individual's or family's decision made with accurate planning. We stand by you on a more intimate level as you explore possibilities, weigh options, and embark on the journey to build an engaging future in the homeland.

## About Us

Repat Armenia is a non-governmental, non-profit institution established in August 2012. Our aim is to promote active repatriation, informing, initiating, and championing the return of high-impact individuals and families.

Our full-time, dedicated staff is based in our Yerevan office near Republic Square.

## How We Help

We are ready to assist you in the following key areas:

- **Consultations**: Online and offline orientation on integration, employment, doing business, networking, education, healthcare, and legal aspects.
- **Information Support**: Practical information and tips about moving, living, and working in Armenia. Access personal experiences and advice from other repatriates via our website and social networks.
- **Employment Support**: Profile scanning, resume editing, and job-matching activities.
- **Networking & Integration**: Informal meetings with fellow repatriates, introductions to organizations, workshops, training sessions, and tours.
- **Medical Insurance**: Access to a special corporate package with a 30% discount for families of 3 or more.
- **Language Courses**: Free Armenian language courses.
- **Concierge Support**: In-depth support program.

## Community Engagement

We pay special attention to raising awareness about repatriation through:
- Forums in key diaspora communities
- Regular online talks and panel discussions
- Influencing government policies to create a pro-repatriation environment

Once you become part of our community, you can engage by sharing your own personal and professional experiences.
