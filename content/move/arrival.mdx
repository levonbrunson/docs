# Arriving in Armenia

You've made it! Most arrivals are via **Zvartnots International Airport** (Yerevan) or **Gyumri Airport**. You may also arrive by land from Georgia or Iran.

## Zvartnots Airport to City Center
The airport is about 20 minutes from the city center. You have the option of taking a taxi, public transportation, or the airport shuttle bus.

### Transportation Options

| Option | Cost | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Taxi** | 2,000 - 6,000 AMD | In case you take personal taxi, make sure you negotiate the final price from the beginning. You can also use taxi apps such as GG, Yandex Taxi, Utaxi to order a car. |
| **Express Bus No. 201** | 300 AMD | Departs every 30 mins (07:00 - 21:30). Complete with WiFi. Takes you to and from Republic Square. You can track the route of the express by clicking [here](https://t-armenia.com/en/yerevan-airport-shuttle-bus). |
| **Public Bus No. 100** | 100 AMD | Connects city center with Zvartnots Airport. From January 1, 2025, cashless payment only via transport cards. You can check fares [here](https://t-armenia.com/en/fares). |

## Arriving by Land

### From Georgia
- **Route**: Via Bagratashen checkpoint.
- **Duration**: ~4 hours to Yerevan (221 km).
- **Train**: If you arrive via train, there is also a train from Tbilisi that takes longer (around 10h) but much more comfortable, bringing you right to the Sasuntsi Davit railway station. Train 371 departs Tbilisi at 20:20 and arrives in Yerevan at 06:55 the next day (operates on odd-numbered days).
- **Cost**: Budget between 7,000 and 25,000 AMD for this trip to and from Tbilisi. Please check the schedules [here](https://www.railway.ge/en/traffic-general-schedule/) or [here](https://t-armenia.com/).

### From Iran
- **Duration**: ~7.5 hours from the border (380 km).
