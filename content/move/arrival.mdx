# Arriving in Armenia

You've arrived in Armenia! Chances are, this was by air at the **Zvartnots International Airport** or **Gyumri Airport**. You may have driven over the Iran or Georgian borders as well.

## Zvartnots Airport to City Center
The airport is about 20 minutes from the city center. You have the option of taking a taxi, public transportation, or the airport shuttle bus.

### Transportation Options

| Option | Cost | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Taxi** | 2,000 - 6,000 AMD | Taxi cost: 2,000-6,000 AMD, depending on the type of taxi, km travelled, etc. In case you take personal taxi, make sure you negotiate the final price from the very beginning. You can also use taxi apps such as [**GG**](https://gg.taxi/en), [**Yandex Taxi**](https://taxi.yandex.com/), [**Utaxi**](https://utaxi.am/) to order a car. |
| **Express Bus No. 201** | 300 AMD | Departing every 30 minutes from 07:00 to 22:00, the shuttle costs 300 AMD, complete with WiFi. It takes you to and from Republic Square. You can track the route of the bus by clicking [here](https://t-armenia.com/en/yerevan-airport-shuttle-bus). |
| **Public Bus No. 100** | 100 AMD | It connects city center with Zvartnots Airport. From January 1, 2025 the new fares apply. You can check them [here](https://t-armenia.com/en/fares). |

## Arriving by Land

If you are planning to arrive by land, there are three transportation routes taking you to Armenia from Georgia, Iran, and Russia. The most optimal road to Armenia from Georgia is via Bagratashen to Yerevan (221 km). If you arrive from Iran, you will have a 7.5 h drive from the border with Armenia to Yerevan (380 km). There is also a train from Tbilisi that takes longer but much more comfortable, bringing you right to the Sasuntsi Davit railway station. Budget between 7,000 and 25,000 AMD is for this trip to and from Tbilisi. Please, check the schedules [here](https://www.railway.ge/en/traffic-general-schedule/) or [here](https://t-armenia.com/).
