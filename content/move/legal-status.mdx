# Legal Status in Armenia

Foreign citizens in Armenia have many rights and obligations. You may work (with a permit), own a business, and rent or own property. You are expected to pay taxes and follow the constitution. However, you cannot vote, be elected, or own agricultural land (with some exceptions).

## Types of Residence Permits

There are three main types of residency status:

| Type | Duration | Cost | Notes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Temporary** | 1 year (extendable) | 105,000 AMD | Requires medical checkup. |
| **Permanent** | 5 years (extendable) | 140,000 AMD | Requires medical checkup. |
| **Special** | 10 years (extendable) | 150,000 AMD | Often for those of Armenian descent. |

### Application Requirements
*   **Application Form**: Includes personal details, family status, etc.
*   **Passport**: Notarized translation + 3 copies.
*   **Photos**: 3 color photos (35x45 mm).
*   **Health Certificate**: From any polyclinic.
*   **Proof of Need**:
    *   *Temporary*: Work, study, marriage, business, or family.
    *   *Permanent*: Close relative with residency/citizenship, business, or 3+ years residence.
*   **Receipt**: Proof of payment.

**Processing Time**: Decisions are made by the Migration and Citizenship Service within 30 days. Renewals should be submitted 30 days before expiration.

## Special Residency (for Armenians)
Issued to foreign citizens of Armenian ancestry.
*   **Benefits**: 10-year validity, visa-free travel to Armenia, full protection of law, exempt from military service.
*   **Proof of Ethnicity**: Baptism certificate, birth certificate of parent/grandparent with ethnicity note, or other official documents.

## Citizenship
Any person 18+ can apply. Dual citizenship is allowed.

### Requirements
*   **Residency**: 3 years of residence (or marriage to a citizen).
*   **Language**: Knowledge of Armenian (Constitution test required).
*   **Ethnicity**: Armenians by descent can apply without residency or language requirements.

### Fees
*   Application: 1,000 AMD
*   Passport (Regular): 1,000 AMD
*   Passport (Biometric): 25,000 AMD
*   ID Card: 3,000 AMD

## Military Service
*   **Compulsory**: Males 18-27 (and recently 27-37 for new citizens) must serve 2 years.
*   **Dual Citizens**: Subject to service unless they served 12+ months in another country's armed forces (or 18 months alternative service) before naturalization.
*   **Exemptions**: Health issues, family circumstances (sole son of fallen soldier), or humanitarian reasons.

## Apostille
Foreign documents used in Armenia (and vice versa) often need an **Apostille** stamp (Hague Convention).
*   **Issued by**: Ministry of Justice (Registry Offices).
*   **Cost**: 7,000 AMD (2-5 days) or higher for expedited service.
