# Entrepreneurship in Armenia

Starting a business in Armenia is relatively straightforward. Foreigners can own 100% of a company, and there are no requirements for local partners or employees.

## Guiding Steps
If you have an idea for a business:
1.  **Develop your idea.**
2.  **Create a business plan** and presentation.
3.  **Do market research.**
4.  **Contact Repat Armenia**: We can put you in touch with professional accounting, legal, and consultancy services, as well as experienced repatriate entrepreneurs.
5.  **Promotion**: We also provide promotion support upon request.

## Registration
*   **Process**: Takes less than a day (with sample documents) or up to 3 days.
*   **Where**: Register at the State Register of Legal Entities (`e-register.am`).
*   **Cost**: No government fees for registration. No minimum capital requirements.
*   **Documents Needed**:
    *   Passport (notarized translation).
    *   Local phone number.
    *   Local address (virtual offices are acceptable).

## Corporate Forms
*   **Private Entrepreneur (PE)**: Simple, good for freelancers.
*   **Limited Liability Company (LLC)**: Most common for small-to-medium businesses.
*   **Joint-Stock Company (JSC)**: For larger enterprises.
*   **Others**: Cooperatives, Branch Offices, Subsidiaries, Non-Profits.

## Opening a Corporate Bank Account
*   **Timeframe**: A few hours to a few days.
*   **Requirements**: Company seal, registration certificate, director/shareholder passports.
*   **Note**: Some banks may have restrictions for citizens of certain countries (e.g., Iran, Syria).

## Free Economic Zones (FEZ)
Residents of FEZs are exempt from most taxes (VAT, corporate, property, customs) for up to 10 years.
*   **Alliance**: Hi-Tech and Pharmaceuticals (Yerevan).
*   **Meridian**: Jewelry and Watchmaking (Yerevan).
*   **Meghri**: Trade with Iran.
*   **ECOS**: Hi-Tech and Blockchain (Hrazdan).

## Government Incentives
*   **IT Startups**: Certified startups pay 0% corporate tax (or 1% turnover tax as of 2025) and 10% income tax on salaries.
*   **Border Regions**: 0% corporate tax, income tax, and VAT in specific territories.
*   **Job Creation**: Up to 30% profit tax reduction for new jobs (for 3 years).
*   **Import/Export**: VAT deferrals for equipment imports; reduced taxes for large exporters.
