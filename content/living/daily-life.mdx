# Daily Life in Armenia

## Lifestyle & Culture
*   **Food**: Seasonal and fresh. Heavy influence from Middle Eastern cuisine. Bread (*lavash*, *matnakash*) and cheese are staples.
*   **Hospitality**: "Bread and salt" is a tradition of welcoming guests.
*   **Safety**: Armenia is one of the safest countries. Violent crime is rare.
*   **Language**:
    *   *Eastern Armenian*: Official language.
    *   *Western Armenian*: Spoken by many in the Diaspora.
    *   *Russian*: Widely understood.
    *   *English*: Growing among youth and in business.

## Useful Apps
*   **Transport**: `Yandex Go`, `GG`, `A2B Transport`, `Yerevan Ride`.
*   **Finance**: `iDram`, `Telcell Wallet`, `EasyPay`.
*   **Shopping/Food**: `Glovo`, `Yandex Eats`, `Menu.am`, `Buy.am`.
*   **Government**: `e-request.am`, `e-citizen.am`.

## Shopping
*   **Markets**: *GUM* (fresh food), *Vernissage* (arts/crafts), *Malatia* (goods/clothes), *Petak* (household).
*   **Malls**: *Dalma Garden*, *Yerevan Mall*, *Rio*, *Mega Mall*.
*   **Online**: `Amazon`/`eBay` via shipping forwarders like *Globbing* or *Onex*.

## Leisure
*   **Parks**: *Victory Park*, *Lovers Park*, *English Park*, *Tumo Park*.
*   **Museums**: *History Museum*, *National Gallery*, *Matenadaran* (manuscripts), *Parajanov Museum*.
*   **Fitness**: Many gyms (*Reebok*, *Gold's Gym*, *Orange Fitness*) and yoga studios.

## Retirement
*   **Age**: 63 years.
*   **Pension**: Basic pension is ~36,000+ AMD. Foreigners with residency/citizenship can qualify if they have worked in Armenia for 10+ years.
