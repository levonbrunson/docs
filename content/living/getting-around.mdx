# Getting Around Town

## Public Transportation
*   **Metro**: Only in Yerevan. Fast and cheap.
*   **Buses/Minibuses**: Extensive network. New buses are being introduced.
*   **Payment**: As of 2025, payment is via **Bank Card** or **Transport Card**. Cash is being phased out.
*   **Apps**: `A2B Transport`, `Yandex Transport`, `Transit.am`.

## Inter-City Travel
*   **Bus Stations**:
    *   *Kilikia*: For West/South directions (Ejmiatsin, Vanadzor, Iran).
    *   *Northern*: For North/East (Sevan, Dilijan).
    *   *Sasuntsi Davit*: For South/East (Ararat, Tbilisi train).
*   **Trains**: Connects Yerevan to Gyumri, Araks, and Tbilisi (Georgia).

## Taxis
*   **Apps**: `GG`, `Yandex Go`, `Utaxi`.
*   **Cost**: Starts ~600-900 AMD for short trips.
*   **Tip**: Avoid street taxis in tourist spots (they overcharge).

## Driving
*   **License**: International Driver's Permits are accepted. Residents may need to convert to a local license.
*   **Traffic**: Can be chaotic. Watch for speed cameras and red light cameras.
*   **Parking**:
    *   **Red Lines**: Paid parking zones (A and B). First 15 minutes are free.
    *   **Payment**: Via SMS, terminals, or apps.

## Micromobility
*   **Scooters**: `Jet`, `Mimo`, `BusyFly`.
*   **Bikes**: `Yerevan Ride`, `Mimo Bike`. New bike lanes are appearing in the center.
